Parvalish

What is Parvalish?
Much like unagi It is not something you do But something you are!

Being parvalish means - Surprising people with breaking window panes to show that it might break
It is being sweet and brilliant and sometimes both on the same date
And having a heart of diamond which loves truly
And cares for everyone around and is friendly!

Being parvalish means being cute enough to make strictest profs like shubhi mam smile
And forgetting what you had been saying just before a little while
It means having the most melodious voice
You are the luckiest if you get to hear it in a day twice

Parvalish means taking things lite even after understanding all emotions
And while explaining something moving your hands in circular motions
It means being nervous about being consistent
But at the same time trying hard is constant.

Footnotes- A person may possess every quality mentioned above.But still, Parvalish is a unique trait only in Prabal Patwa. And that makes him special and irreplacable.
